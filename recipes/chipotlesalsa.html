Chipotle Salsa

A smokey, dark red salsa that enchants the tastebuds of all who encounter it.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Roma Tomatoes
  • Canned Crushed Tomatoes
  • Canned Chipotle
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • White Onion
  • Poblano Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop cilantro (include stems).
  2. Pour crushed tomatoes into a food processor with chipotle and pulse until smooth.
  3. Cut other ingredients into small enough pieces for a food processor.
  4. Pulse all ingredients together in food processor until it arrives at the consistency you desire.
  5. Serve up smiles!
