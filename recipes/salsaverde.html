Salsa Verde

A lovely, bright green salsa whose primary ingredient is tomatillos, rather than the typical base of tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tomatillos
  • Avocado
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro
  • White Onion

Steps

  1. Chop cilantro (include stems).
  2. Cut other ingredients into small enough pieces for a food processor.
  3. Pulse all ingredients together in food processor until it arrives at the consistency you desire.
  4. Serve up smiles!
